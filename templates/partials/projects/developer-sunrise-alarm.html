Sunrise Alarm

What is this?

It's an alarm clock - that rings when the sun comes up.

Think of this as a replacement for your rooster in the backyard

Nothing beats binary in the morning

Turning off this sunrise alarm is just as difficult as chasing your rooster around.

Snoozing the alarm is as simple as pushing a button

However, to turn off the alarm, the user has to convert a number from binary to hexidecimal.

Crazy? The team wanted to ensure that the user would wake up and we thought this should do it.

Under the Hood

Tools used include:

  • Arduino UNO
  • Arduino time library
  • Photoresistors
  • Tri-LEDs
  • Speakers
  • & other hardware
