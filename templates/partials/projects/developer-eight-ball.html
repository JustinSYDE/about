8 Ball

A Mind Reader

8 Ball is a mind reader iOS app.

Have your mind read by installing the app (link below).

Flash Mind Reader

This app's algorithm is based off the notorious Flash Mind Reader game that ruled the internet when it first came out

I remember flipping tables because I couldn't figure out how it was doing it's magic.

My goal was to create a similar app with a different user interface while maintaining that same creepiness of the original mind reader app.

Under the Hood

Tools used include:

  • Arduino UNO
  • Arduino time library
  • Photoresistors
  • Tri-LEDs
  • Speakers
  • & other hardware
