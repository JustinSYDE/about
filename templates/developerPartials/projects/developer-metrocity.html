Metrocity

What is Metrocity?

Torontonians and visitors,

Trouble deciding on either buying a TTC Metropass or just sticking to tokens?

Metrocity helps you decide which is the best solution for you.

This Doesn't Exist Already?

I was shocked to find that such an app did not exist.

When I worked at a bank downtown, I communuted occasionaly and I had access to student discounts to the Metropass.

It took me a fair amount of research and time to figure out what to purchase.

I figured I wasn't alone on this issue - so I built Metrocity!
