Zone Six

Landscaping Company

Zone Six Design Build Ltd. is a landscaping company founded in 1994 in Toronto, Canada.

They have worked with firms including Corban and Goode, Ferris & Associates Inc, William Dewson Architects & many more.

The company provides softscape and hardscape services for both residential and commercial consumers.

Specialty services include woodwork and stonework

Old to the New

Zone Six made plans to update their former website to something more modern, minimalistic and fun.

My job was to design and build this new website.

Other responsibilities included designing a new company logo and editing the portfolio photos to be uploaded to the website.

The new website is responsive, mobile & tablet-friendly and sports a modern look.
