Don't Rush

A Toilet Game

Don't Rush is a simple swiping game to have you think before you swipe right. Unlock new levels and challenges with new high scores!

Try it by installing the app (link below).

My First iPhone App

This was my opportunity to learn the iOS SDK and to gain experience with managing the entire lifecycle of an app on the AppStore.

Ran interviews and user testing with Tinderers and non-tinderers to improve gameplay, game mechanics, and UI/UX.

Gained exposure and got involved with the indie dev community via /r/IndieDev.
