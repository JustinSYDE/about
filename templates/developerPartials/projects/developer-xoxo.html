XOXO

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe

No - not just 3x3 tic-tac-toe.

XOXO features 4x4 single and double player modes.

Story behind XOXO

I simply wanted to play 4x4 tic-tac-toe where you could still win with 3 in a row.

Winning with 4 in a row is really hard - even on easy mode.
